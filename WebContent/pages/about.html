ABOUT

TWB

"Finding a customs broker has never been so easy. I can choose from a list of qualified brokers or, do it all myself with the help of the TWB Forms Assistant"

Trade Without Boundaries simplifies the import and export process by helping Small to Medium sized businesses streamline the process by utilizing document conversion and relationship extraction technology to examine given information and auto-populate relevant customs forms for the Client.

We offer a marketplace where Customs Brokers can offer their services to Small and Medium sized businesses. TWB is a platform for Brokers to join a Customs Broker Network and be recruited, allowing Clients and Customs Brokers work more efficiently together to save time and money while facilitating importing and exporting across the globe.

    OUR VISION:

    Make international trade easy

    Services Offered for Importers and Exporters:

    Forms Assistant

    Clients can upload their products technical specifications and provide additional details that will be used to auto-populate all the required forms that are relevant to the shipment, location and destination of import and export. Forms can be archived for future reference to expedite the import and export process. Clients can also access the Custom Broker Network to help complete the transaction or submit their forms electronically to the appropriate government agencies.
    Key Features:
    -Document extraction and automatic population of customs forms
    -Electronic sharing of export information with DHS
    -Document archiving
    -Rating system for Customs Brokers

    Services Offered for Customs Brockers

    Customs Broker Network

    Customs Brokers can access pre-populated customs forms from clients that require a Customs Broker's expertise for importing and exporting. They can save time by having the work partially completed, which allows them to complete more jobs and charge thier customers less.
    Key Features:
    Access to small to medium size clients through the Customs Broker Network
    Pre-completed customs forms to reduce administration time
